ABOUT FUNCLES

WELCOME TO FUNCLES

Funcles(Fun-Uncles) is a GENESIS collection of 3333 unique funcles living on the Ethereum Blockchain. He likes to have fun, enjoy life, and grow his wicked Mo to support Movember.

Join our adventure in support of Men's Health and explore the web3 revolution while creating meaningful memories with our friends along the way.

A SYMBOL FOR MEN’S HEALTH.

Funcles' artwork illustrates the authenticity and struggles of men’s health, both physical and mental. Men’s health is much more than having a muscular physique- it’s also about balancing a healthy lifestyle and maintaining a healthy mind space. With the dad bod, we hope to normalize and promote acceptance of the different and natural body types while still being conscious of healthy habits. He also sports a Movember Mustache to symbolize his support for the men’s health movement. Funcles is committed to creating a community within our dope ecosystem.
